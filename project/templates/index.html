About this page

The purpose of this website is to provide information about how various networks in the brain influence psychopathological disorders.

We often have a vision of the brain as a sort of puzzle, with each brain region assigned to a different task. A better way to think of the brain is as a series of complex networks and systems. These systems are responsible for some of our most important functions, such as attention, action, and emotion.

Psychopathology is a great way to allow people to engage with the brain, as it is something that almost anyone could relate to. We all know someone who has experienced an instance of psychopathology; in this way, it is relevant to all of us. Learning about the networks of the brain through the lens of psychopathology allows the brain to become more relevant to us from a neuroscientific standpoint.
